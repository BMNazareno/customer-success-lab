# Health Score Project

## Business Problem

Customer Success Managers spend significant time identifying which accounts require proactive attention.

As customer portfolios grow, manually reviewing login activity, support tickets and satisfaction metrics becomes increasingly time-consuming and inconsistent.

This project builds a simple health scoring model that prioritizes accounts using customer engagement indicators.


## Business Goal

- Reduce manual prioritization effort
- Increase proactive outreach
- Reduce weekly account review time.
- Standardize account prioritization.
- Help Customer Success Managers identify at-risk accounts earlier.

## Data

- Last login
- NPS
- Open tickets
- Active users

## Target Audience

Customer Success and Operations practitioners seeking hands-on experience with account prioritization automation.

## Dataset Assumptions

- NPS values use a 0–100 scale.
- `DaysSinceLastLogin` represents customer engagement.
- `RenewalDays` indicates the remaining days until renewal.
- `ExpectedHealth` is the expected classification used to validate the scoring algorithm.
